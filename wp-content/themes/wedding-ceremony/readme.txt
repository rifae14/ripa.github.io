== Description ==

The Wedding Ceremony WordPress Theme is the perfect solution for couples, planners, and professionals aiming to create a captivating digital experience for their big day. This theme is ideal for showcasing every detail of a wedding ceremony, including wedding venues, wedding themes, and wedding flowers, while also highlighting wedding invitations, wedding rings, and wedding dresses. Whether you’re planning a destination wedding, an outdoor wedding ceremony, or a traditional church wedding, this theme caters to various styles and preferences. It allows you to feature essential elements like wedding arches, wedding decorations, and even wedding cakes, while ensuring a smooth user experience for exploring wedding catering options, seating charts, and wedding day timelines. The customizable design enables you to reflect your wedding colour schemes, wedding budget, and wedding ideas effortlessly. Its responsive layout makes it easy for guests to RSVP, explore luxury wedding ceremony details, or check out wedding entertainment options such as wedding music, wedding bands, or wedding DJs. Dedicated sections for wedding photography, wedding videography, and wedding favours add a touch of personalization, while high-resolution imagery of wedding moments enhances visual appeal. Whether it’s a rustic wedding ceremony, a modern celebration, or a grand luxury event, this theme supports all needs, including creating detailed schedules for the wedding reception or post-wedding brunch. With the Wedding Ceremony WordPress Theme, you can craft an unforgettable online presence, ensuring your wedding day is beautifully presented and celebrated by all. 

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
